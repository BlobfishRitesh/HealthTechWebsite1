About Us

Small local club from Centreville High School in the Northern Virginia Fairfax County region. We are dedicated to improving people's health through technology and innovation. Our current main project focuses on helping blind people navigate through their environment using advanced sensory technologies. We will continue to explore new ways to leverage technology for the betterment of society.
